Flex: Grades K-2

This camp is very enjoyable for children ages 5-7, because it is fun, exciting, and educational at the same time.

On the first day of camp, students will perfect their art skills, while also learning to draw some very fun things, such as cars and dragons.

The second day of camp is equally fun, and is taught by the same teacher from day one, who happens to be a skilled player of the violin.

In the music class, kids will learn to control the volume, pitch, and sound of their voices.

The third day of camp is very exciting, requires a child with lots of energy, and is super fun!

This camp is probably the most useful, with children learning to speak in front of an audience. Whether they are practicing to give a huge speech,

or trying to overcome stage fright, these kids will soon be amazing public speakers!

And all of this completely free! Three consecutive days of happiness, all for the price of fun!
